About the author

Kent Ntwari was born in Bujumbura, the capital city of Burundi, where he spent eighteen fervent years learning how to minister effectively the gospel of Jesus-Christ. Happily married and a servant of Redeemed Gospel Ministries in Canada under the leadership of Bishop Innocent Rugagi, he continues his work to faithfully spread the word of God around the world.
